Outstanding Young Farmer & Rancher Contest

One winner is chosen from each of the 13 Texas Farm Bureau districts. Following a second round of judging, three finalists will be selected. After an on-farm visit, they will be our guests in San Antonio in December, where one final winner will be announced at the annual meeting. Expenses will also be paid for the state winner to travel to compete in the national contest at the American Farm Bureau Federation Annual Meeting.

Eligibility

  • Must be a Texas Farm Bureau member actively engaged in farming and/or ranching.
  • Applicant and spouse shall not have reached their 36th birthday on or before Jan. 31, 2017.
  • If a spouse is ineligible because of age, an eligible applicant may still enter the contest.
  • All information concerning the ineligible spouse must be excluded from the application.
  • There is no limit on the number of entries from each county.
